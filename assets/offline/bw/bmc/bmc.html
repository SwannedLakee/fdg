Vinaya Pitaka - Buddhist Monastic Code

Intro &
Abbreviations BMC 1
  BMC 2
 

 

Buddhist Monastic Code

BMC1 is primarily concerned with the Pāṭimokkha rules while BMC2 is primarily concerned with the Khandhaka rules although there is no clear demarcation between the two volumes and much of the material overlaps.
