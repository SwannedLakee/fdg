Pārājikas Sanghādisesas Nissaggiya Pācittiyas Pācittiyas Pāṭidesanīyas Sekhiyas Adhikaraṇa-Samathās

Bhikkhuni Vibhanga - Sekhiya 28: Maintain the attention on the bowl when going to collect food.

  • Translated from the Pali by I.B. Horner.

1At that time the enlightened one, the lord was staying at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove in Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery. Now at that time the group of six monks accepted almsfood looking about here and there; they did not know that they were piled up and overflowing … " …

2‘Thinking of the bowl will I accept almsfood,’ is a training to be observed."

3One should accept almsfood thinking of the bowl. Whoever out of disrespect accepts almsfood, looking about here and there, there is an offence of wrong-doing.

4There is no offence if it is unintentional … if he is the first wrong-doer.
