Key Suttas on the Topic

  • mn83 — 14, 15 and 8 days.
  • mn146 — 14th waxing moon, 15th full moon.
  • an3.37 — comparison of the 14th, 15th, and 8th days with deities.
  • an3.70 — "nights and days" rather than "days and nights"; the Pali has the fixed expression ahoratta (“day-night”) — in other contexts.
