Key Features of Dhamma.gift

What initiatives make up Dhamma.gift?

Dhamma.Gift

"Swiss Army Knife" or A One-stop Dhamma Portal

Dhamma.gift was created with the idea of consolidating and aggregating top Dhamma and Pali-related online resources, such as Pali texts, important websites, dictionaries, ebooks, and tools.

You can find them in the Collection of Links in the Toolbar under the search input. Feel free to suggest any missing resources.

Open


Find.Dhamma.Gift

Sutta-Vinaya Search

Designed for those attempting to systematize information from Pali Suttas and Vinaya. It provides search results in convenient tables, available for both basic and advanced filtering, various sorting options and grouping by matched texts and word variations for deep researches.

Search results offer direct access to canonical texts, with line-by-line Pali and English (and Russian) translations. Includes various external links such as DigitalPaliReader.online, TheBuddhasWords.net, SuttaCentral.net, Theravada.ru and Theravada.su.

Example search for "turtle" (kacchapa) in Four Main Nikayas: https://find.dhamma.gift/?q=kacchap


Dhamma.Gift Read

Read Pali texts with "line-by-line" translation and a popup dictionary

For English speakers:
  • Offers a quick and straightforward interface for reading.
  • The only site offering such a wide variety of Digital Pali Dictionary (DPD) lookup options for Pali. And even ability to use any other dictionary within mobile application DictTango.
    • Full-scale click-to-lookup DPD online, with all the information available on dpdict.net.
    • Compact version of DPD online.
    • Lightweight built-in DPD version, similar to those on TBW and SC.
    • Exclusive direct lookup in the DictTango Android app.
    • Extra mode: launch dhamma.gift search instantly by double-clicking a word.
  • Unique reading modes for Pali: "No punctuation," "For memorization," and "Study Non-Latin script helper" for Devanagari or Thai.
  • Hotkeys for navigation and settings.

Open Table of Content https://dhamma.gift/read.php

For Russian speakers:
  • The only website offering such a large volume of "line-by-line" Pali-Russian texts.
  • Includes AN, DN, MN, SN, and KN (Snp, Ud, Iti, Dhp, Thag, Thig), along with partially Vinaya Vibhaṅga.
  • Multilanguage mode: Pali - Russian - English.
  • The only site with a built-in Pali-Russian DPD Online On-click Lookup.

Example: SN 1.1 https://dhamma.gift/ru/sn1.1

General Principles and Features

  • Developed and optimized for smartphones and tablets, while also working seamlessly on desktop browsers.
  • The popup dictionary plugin can be integrated into any website. Description here.
  • Actively developed, with feedback welcomed.
  • Privacy is a priority: no user data is collected, all settings are stored in the browser.
  • Can be installed and run locally on Android, Windows or Mac for Offline usage.
