Key Features of Dhamma.gift

What initiatives make up Dhamma.gift?

Dhamma.Gift

"Swiss Army Knife" or A One-stop Dhamma Portal

Dhamma.gift was created with the idea of consolidating and aggregating top Dhamma and Pali-related online resources, such as Pali texts, important websites, dictionaries, ebooks, and tools.

You can find them in the Collection of Links in the Toolbar under the search input. Feel free to suggest any missing resources.

Open


Find.Dhamma.Gift

Sutta-Vinaya Search

Designed for those attempting to systematize information in Pali Suttas and Vinaya. It provides search results in convenient tables, available for both basic and advanced filtering, various sorting options and grouping by matched texts and word variations for deep researches.

Search results offer direct access to canonical texts, with line-by-line Pali and English (and Russian) translations. Includes various external links such as DPR, SC, TBW, theravada.ru, and theravada.su.

Example search for "turtle" (kacchapa): https://find.dhamma.gift/?q=kacchap


Dhamma.Gift Read

Read Pali texts with interlinear translation and a popup dictionary

For English speakers:
  • The only site offering such a wide variety of DPD lookup options for Pali.
  • Full-scale click-to-lookup DPD online, with all the information available on dpdict.net.
  • Lightweight built-in DPD version, similar to those on TBW and SC.
  • Exclusive direct lookup in the DictTango Android app.
  • Extra mode: launch find.dhamma.gift search instantly by double-clicking a word.
  • Unique reading modes for Pali: "No punctuation," "For memorization," and "Non-Latin script helper" for Devanagari or Thai.
  • Hotkeys for navigation and settings.
  • Offers a quick and straightforward interface for reading.

Example: MN 118 https://dhamma.gift/mn118

For Russian speakers:
  • The only website offering such a large volume of line-by-line Pali-Russian texts.
  • Includes AN, DN, MN, SN, and KN (Snp, Ud, Iti, Dhp, Thag, Thig), along with partial Vinaya Vibhaá¹…ga.
  • Multilanguage mode: Pali - Russian - English.
  • The only site with a built-in Pali-Russian DPD Online On-click Lookup.

Example: SN 1.1 https://dhamma.gift/ru/sn1.1

General Principles and Features

  • Developed and optimized for use on smartphones and tablets, but also supports desktop browsers.
  • The popup dictionary plugin can be integrated into any website. Description here.
  • Actively developed, with feedback welcomed.
  • Privacy is a priority: no user data is collected, all settings are stored in the browser.
  • Can be installed and run locally on Android, Windows, or Mac for Offline usage.
