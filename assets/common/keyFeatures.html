Key Features of Dhamma.gift

What initiatives make up Dhamma.gift?

Dhamma.Gift

A Dhamma multi-tool or one-window portal

Dhamma.gift was created to gather in one place the best online resources on Dhamma and Pali, including texts, dictionaries, important websites, e-books and tools.

All of them are available in the "Collection of Links" in the toolbar under the search field. If you think something is missing, let us know!

Go to or get more details on the Dhamma-resources here


Find.Dhamma.Gift

Sutta and Vinaya Search

This tool is designed for those who want to systematize information from Pali Suttas and Vinaya. Search results are presented in convenient tables with basic and advanced filtering options, various sorting options and the ability to group by texts and by words for comprehensive study.

The search provides direct access to Pali texts with line-by-line translations in English and Russian and external links to: DigitalPaliReader.online, TheBuddhasWords.net, SuttaCentral.net, Theravada.ru and Theravada.su.

Example: find everything about turtles (kacchapa) in the Four Main Nikayas: https://find.dhamma.gift/?q=kacchap


Dhamma.Gift Read

Read Pali texts with line-by-line Russian and English translations and a pop-up dictionary

For English speakers:

  • The only site offering such a wide variety of DPD connection options. And other dictionaries through the DictTango Android and Mdict IOS mobile apps.
    • Full-fledged DPD online search, including all information from dpdict.net.
    • Compact version of DPD online.
    • Lightweight built-in version of DPD, similar to TBW and SC.
    • Direct connection of DictTango Android and Mdict IOS.
    • Additional mode: instant search on find.dhamma.gift when double-clicking a word.
  • Unique Pali reading modes: "No punctuation", "For memorization", "Reading assistant for non-Latin writing systems" (for those studying Devanagari, Thai and other scripts).
  • Hotkeys for convenient navigation.

Read mn118 https://dhamma.gift/mn118

For Russian speakers:

  • The only site with such a volume of line-by-line Pali-Russian texts.
  • Includes AN, DN, MN, SN, KN (Snp, Ud, Iti, Dhp, Thag, Thig), as well as partially Vinaya Vibhaṅga.
  • Multilingual mode: Pali - Russian - English.
  • The only site with a built-in and Online Russian version of Digital Pali Dictionary (DPD) - a pop-up dictionary on click.

General Principles and Features

  • Developed and optimized for smartphones and tablets, in addition to basic work in desktop browsers.
  • The project is actively developing, feedback is welcome.
  • Privacy is a priority: no user data is collected, all settings are stored in the browser.
  • The pop-up dictionary plugin on click can be integrated into any site. Description here.
  • For offline access, all parts of the project can be installed locally on Android, Windows, Mac.
