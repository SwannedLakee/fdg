pli-tv-kd2 2. Uposathakkhandhaka The chapter on the observance day
dantakaṭṭhena
 2 3 SC

Tehi, bhikkhave, bhikkhūhi so bhikkhu saṅgahetabbo anuggahetabbo upalāpetabbo upaṭṭhāpetabbo cuṇṇena mattikāya dantakaṭṭhena mukhodakena.
Those monks should treat that learned monk with kindness. They should assist him and support him, and they should attend on him with bath powder, soap, tooth cleaners, and water for rinsing the mouth.
No ce saṅgaṇheyyuṁ anuggaṇheyyuṁ upalāpeyyuṁ upaṭṭhāpeyyuṁ cuṇṇena mattikāya dantakaṭṭhena mukhodakena, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
If they don’t look after him in this way, they commit an offense of wrong conduct.

pli-tv-kd15 15. Khuddakavatthukkhandhaka The chapter with short topics
dantakaṭṭhan dantakaṭṭhassa dantakaṭṭhaṁ dantakaṭṭhāni
 13 48 SC

Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhū dantakaṭṭhaṁ na khādanti.
At that time the monks did not clean their teeth.
“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā dantakaṭṭhassa akhādane.
“There are these five disadvantages of not cleaning your teeth:
ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā dantakaṭṭhassa akhādane.

Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṁsā dantakaṭṭhassa khādane.
There are these five benefits of cleaning your teeth:
ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṁsā dantakaṭṭhassa khādane.

Anujānāmi, bhikkhave, dantakaṭṭhan”ti.
I allow tooth cleaners.”
Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū dīghāni dantakaṭṭhāni khādanti, teheva sāmaṇeraṁ ākoṭenti.
The monks from the group of six used long tooth cleaners and even used them to hit the novice monks.
“Na, bhikkhave, dīghaṁ dantakaṭṭhaṁ khāditabbaṁ.
“You should not use long tooth cleaners.
Anujānāmi, bhikkhave, aṭṭhaṅgulaparamaṁ dantakaṭṭhaṁ, na ca tena sāmaṇero ākoṭetabbo.
I allow tooth cleaners that are a maximum of thirteen centimeters long. And you should not use them to hit the novice monks.
Tena kho pana samayena aññatarassa bhikkhuno atimaṭāhakaṁ dantakaṭṭhaṁ khādantassa kaṇṭhe vilaggaṁ hoti.
On one occasion a monk used a tooth cleaner that was too short and it got stuck in his throat.
“Na, bhikkhave, atimaṭāhakaṁ dantakaṭṭhaṁ khāditabbaṁ.
“You should not use tooth cleaners that are too short.
Anujānāmi, bhikkhave, caturaṅgulapacchimaṁ dantakaṭṭhan”ti.
You should not use tooth cleaners shorter than seven centimeters.”
dantakaṭṭhaṁ ākoṭane.
