pli-tv-bu-vb-pc53 53. Hasadhammasikkhāpada 53. The training rule on playing
water with his hand with his foot with a stick
 1 0 SC

Hatthena vā pādena vā kaṭṭhena vā kaṭhalāya vā udakaṁ paharati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
If he strikes the water with his hand, with his foot, with a stick, or with a stone, he commits an offense of wrong conduct.

“This is the bed, this the bench, this the mattress, this the pillow, this the place for defecating, this the place for urinating, this the water for drinking, this the water for washing, this the walking stick; these are the Sangha’s agreements concerning the right time to enter and the right time to leave.”
